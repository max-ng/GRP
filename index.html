General Route Planner

Using AI to Maximize Logistics

GRP(General Route Planner) is a high-performance and open-source routing engine for multiple pickup/drop-offs routing optimization and multi-vehicle allocation problem. The algorithm is designed to work faster and finding better cost minimum than genetic algorithms, tabu search and ant colony optimization in this problem.

Online Demo

Each order contains a pickup location and a drop-off location.

Click on the map to add the pickup and drop-off locations. Then click "calucate". For quick demonstration, just click the "quick demo with 10 stops" button.

GRP is able to solved:

  • multiple pickup/dropoffs routing optimization: N to N delievey / 1 to N delivery / with or without parcel limit constraint.
  • multi-vehicle routing problem: with or without parcel limit constraint.
